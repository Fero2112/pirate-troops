THE GAME

Pirate Troops mixes point and click immersive story telling with fun card collection & fighting mechanics. Puzzles and mystery solving will lead to unlocking cards and recruiting crew mates!

Sammy is a pirate kid who lives on the island of Tortuga Gorda with his Gramps. He will depart to adventure in search of his Father, who was once one of the mightiest pirates ever known but disappeared under mysterious circumstances.

SOLVE PUZZLES

Pirate Troops is full of puzzles with a lot of inspiration of old point and click classics.

GATHER CARDS & FIGHT

Collect cards, develop strategies and win Fights!

RECRUIT THE CREW

Put together a crew of uncanny characters. You will find pirates, corsairs, monsters and even animals who will join Sammy's quest

SAMMY SHORTLEGS

Sammy is the protagonist of this story. He is a naive young pirate boy with great ambitions! He won't stop at anything to find out what happened to his father.

GRAMPS LUCIUS SHORTLEGS

Gramps was once a fearsome pirate and legends about him are still told, but he is at the end of his days. He wants to protect Sammy, but the family treasure of the Shortlegs is starting to run low.

BAXTER PARROTFACE

Every great pirate has a magical parrot who will accompany him through the sea and the storms. Baxter may not be the typical magical parrot kind, but he is Sammy's
